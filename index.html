About IT Department

The Department of Information Technology was established in 2001 to cater to the growing demands of technically sound professionals in the IT industry. This program is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation with effect from the Academic Year 2019-2024.

Initially, it started with just 30 seats in the first academic year. However, in the following years, the total intake is 60 seats per year. The department has qualified senior faculty with the state of holistic, socially conscious to facilitate efficient teaching learning process.

The department is headed by (Mrs.) Sunantha Guruswamy. Currently, there are 08 faculty members in Information Technology. The department has six well equipped computer labs with the latest computer systems, with open-source software, and an internet facility through a good broadband wifi system.

All classrooms & labs are well-resourced with amenities such as projector, internet facility. The department has consistently achieved 96% results in academics and with 5% students always above 95%, 93%, 92% in BE, TE, SE respectively.

Also, 70% placement, 25% for higher education with >60 % undergoing internship on weekdays placements is done with companies like CapGemini, Accenture, E&Y, TCS and more.

About College

Don Bosco Institute of Technology (DBIT), established in 2001, is located in Kurla (W), Mumbai. The institute is affiliated to the University of Mumbai and approved by AICTE.

DBIT aims to provide quality technical education with a focus on values, innovation, and holistic development. The campus offers modern infrastructure, skilled faculty, active student clubs, and excellent placement opportunities.

With a strong emphasis on research, entrepreneurship, and community engagement, DBIT has emerged as a preferred choice for engineering aspirants in Mumbai.
